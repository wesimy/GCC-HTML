Welcome To GCC National Oil Companies Steering Committe

The Steering Committee of the GCC National Oil Companies was established in 1997 by the following companies: Abu Dhabi National Oil Company (ADNOC), Bahrain Petroleum Company (BAPCO), Kuwait Oil Company (KOC), Saudi Aramco, Petroleum Development Oman (PDO) and Qatar Petroleum (QP). The main objective of the committee is to encourage cooperation and knowledge sharing among the nation oil companies in the Gulf in exploration and production areas. To this end, five sub-committees were formed namely: Maintenance & Production, Drilling, Materials & Supply, Exploration & Development and HSE.
